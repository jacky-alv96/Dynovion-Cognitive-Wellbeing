Individual Therapy

What is Individual Therapy?

PERMA Mental Health provides a complete range of in-person and tele-health therapy services that help you understand your challenges, modify your behavior and learn healthy coping skills. Our friendly mental health counselors have specialized training in helping adults, teens, and children. Our team is experienced, dedicated and understanding

Individual therapy is for people dealing with:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Trauma
  • Stress or burnout
  • Grief of Loss
  • Anger Management Issues

"We help people untangle their lives and strengthen their relationships, and are thrilled that you are considering becoming one of our valued clients. If you are feeling like something's got to give but you don't know what - reach out."

“We Listen”

With decades of experience and a deep commitment to personalized care, we support individuals, couples, and families through life's most complex challenges. Whether facing addiction, trauma, or health-related stress, Dr. Timothy Hoesktra at Dynovion Cognitive Wellbeing offers trusted, compassionate guidance—helping you heal, grow, and move forward with clarity and confidence.

About Dr. Thimothy Hoesktra

Timothy Hoekstra is a Licensed Clinical Professional Counselor in Idaho and a Licensed Mental Health Counselor in Washington. Since 1998, he has helped individuals overcome addiction and life challenges with empathy and expertise. His background includes developing treatment programs and supporting families facing serious medical conditions during his clinical training in Spokane.

Transforming your healthcare

Mr. Hoekstra believes that clients are the experts of their own lives and possess the internal wisdom needed to reach their full potential. He acknowledges that along the path of growth and personal development, individuals may encounter obstacles where professional help is necessary. As humans are fundamentally designed for relationships, it is within this context that we move toward our greatest selves.

Respecting all beliefs

While Mr. Hoekstra does not adhere to any specific religion, he has successfully worked with individuals from various faiths and sects. He respects the beliefs of his clients and collaborates with them to enhance their values while working towards resolution, insight, and healing. He sees his role as a counselor as being "a facilitator of insight."

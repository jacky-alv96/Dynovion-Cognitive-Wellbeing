Adolescent Therapy

What is Adolescent Therapy?

Adolescent therapy is a type of counseling focused on helping teenagers (typically ages 12–18) navigate emotional, behavioral, and mental health challenges. It provides a safe space for teens to express themselves, build coping skills, and improve relationships with family and peers.

Common issues addressed:

  • Anxiety or depression
  • Stress from school or social pressures
  • Identity, self-steem, or body image concerns.
  • Family conflict or divorce
  • Trauma or grief
  • Risky behaviors (substance use, self-harm, etc)

"We offer compassionate support for individuals, couples, and families facing life's challenges. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, addiction, trauma, or relationship issues, we create a safe space for healing, insight, and growth. Let's take the next step together."

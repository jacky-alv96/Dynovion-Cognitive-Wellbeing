Our Services Include:

Individual Therapy

Timothy offers compassionate one-on-one counseling for individuals navigating personal challenges such as anxiety, depression, PTSD, grief, addiction, and life transitions. Drawing from a broad therapeutic background, he provides a safe space for self-exploration and healing.

Learn More

Couples & Relationship Counseling

Through thoughtful and respectful guidance, Timothy helps couples improve communication, rebuild trust, deepen intimacy, and resolve long-standing conflicts. His relational approach supports partners in gaining clarity, expressing needs, and strengthening emotional bonds—whether they're seeking to heal, grow, or simply reconnect more meaningfully.

Learn More

Family Therapy

Timothy works with families of all shapes and sizes to improve communication, reduce tension, and foster healthier relationships. Whether addressing adolescent behavioral concerns, family conflict, or adjusting to major life events, he helps families gain insight into their dynamics and establish collaborative, supportive patterns that promote long-term wellbeing.

Learn More

Adolescent Counseling

With deep sensitivity to the developmental challenges of adolescence, Timothy supports teens facing anxiety, identity confusion, academic stress, behavioral struggles, and emotional overwhelm. His work with adolescents helps them develop coping skills, self-awareness, and confidence in navigating life's pressures and forming healthy relationships.
